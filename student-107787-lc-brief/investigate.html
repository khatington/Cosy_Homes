Context of Brief

A Smart home is referred to a home equipped with appliances that can fulfill tasks that normally humans can do. Tasks such as turning on the heating, lights, maximizing security in the house and several other devices. Smart homes are seen as efficient and energy-saving as they make living much easier and simpler. Homeowners can easily interact and operate systems through websites, controls, switches, and voiceovers without any sort of difficulty. There are several advantages of owning a smart home let alone owning smart devices such as being able to manage all your home devices in one place. In one tap of a button or a swipe from your iPad, you can connect and control your house easily and accessibly. Smart home systems have flexible appliances and devices to accommodate not only indoors but outdoor spaces. This allows you to upgrade to a lifestyle of technology. A beneficial advantage to owning a smart home is that it maximizes home security. Due to devices being flexible and accessible, you can receive various alerts during the day, and monitor activities in your house whether you are inside the house or outside somewhere else.

- Control air temperature by voice commands
- An embedded Alexa Amazon to digital devices like turning on the TV
- High quality cameras for security that records automatically based on a set time or when you're out of the house
